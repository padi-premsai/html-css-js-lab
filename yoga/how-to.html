How to Start Your Yoga Journey

Gentle steps toward peace, strength, and self-awareness

Beginner's Guide

  1. Start Slow: Begin with beginner-friendly classes or home practice. Focus on alignment and breathing.
  2. Set a Routine: Practice consistently, even just 10–15 minutes a day.
  3. Choose a Style: Explore different types (like Hatha, Vinyasa, or Yin) to find what fits you best.
  4. Use Props: Don’t hesitate to use blocks, straps, or cushions for support.
  5. Stay Patient: Progress takes time. Honor your body and rest when needed.

Recommended Poses for Beginners

  • 🧘‍♀️ Mountain Pose (Tadasana)
  • 🧘‍♀️ Child’s Pose (Balasana)
  • 🧘‍♀️ Downward Dog (Adho Mukha Svanasana)
  • 🧘‍♀️ Cat-Cow Stretch (Marjaryasana/Bitilasana)
  • 🧘‍♀️ Seated Forward Bend (Paschimottanasana)
